About Me





Hi! My name is Luke Pielak and I am currently a Grade 12 student enrolled at Holy Cross Regional Secondary School. This website is a project for my programming 12 class. While the content of my site is not unified by any specific theme, every page has to do with something I'm interested in. For example, some of my hobbies/interests include esports, music, classical piano, and programming. In my free time, I love to play heavily skill-based video games like Valorant, Smash-Bros, and Osu. Additionally, I enjoy watching Twitch, my favorite streamers are Atrioc, sinatraa, and xQc; and I also love listening to classical music, my favorite composers are Chopin and Liszt and my favorite piece right now is Scherzo no.1 in B minor by Chopin. As I stress over school, extra-curriculars, and my future, it's these things that allow me to enjoy life and look forward to each day.

When I grow up I'm hoping to become a software-developer for a big tech company like Nvidia, Google, or Microsoft. However, I can also see myself becoming a game developer for a company like Riot Games; this especially interests me as I have a passion for esports. I'm applying to universities right now and I really hope I get admitted to UBC for Computer Science. Although it will difficult and a degree of luck will be involved, I believe that education at UBC will give me the best opportunity to pursue my future goals and aspirations.

Anyways, I hope you enjoy my site and feel free to check out my Instagram as well. I don't post very often, but to give incentive, I have cool things planned for the future of my account. Have a nice day!
